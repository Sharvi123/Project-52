Panda

Panda Interesting Facts

  1. A giant panda is much bigger than your teddy bear.
  2. Pandas go from pink to white and black
  3. An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Camel


Camel Interesting Facts

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
